About New Logo

A technology business called New Logo creates cross-platform applications for desktop and mobile devices, with a product portfolio that includes solutions for macOS, Windows, Linux, Android, iOS, and iPadOS.

Quang Lam founded New Logo in 2014, and the business was formally incorporated in August of that same year. We have helped tens of thousands of users worldwide ever since we started.

Our goal is to create cross-platform software that streamlines, improves, and organizes daily activities.
