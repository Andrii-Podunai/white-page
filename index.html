Desktop Apps

Desktop apps, also known as site-specific browsers (SSB), let you launch your favorite web services quickly in self-contained, distraction-free windows.

Let's not bother with bothersome browser tab switching! Desktop applications supplement websites with a variety of features to increase productivity and workflow, such as:

Multiple Accounts

Manage several online services accounts at once and switch between them with a single click or keyboard shortcut.

Menu Bar Apps

Attach apps to menu bar (or tray) for quick access.

Protocol Handlers

Set the default email link handler to your preferred webmail service, such as Gmail or Fastmail. Alternately, configure Google Calendar to handle web calendar links.

App Lock

Passwords or Touch ID can help you keep prying eyes out of your data.

Spaces

Spaces allow you to organize similar web apps into orderly collections and access them quickly with all the advantages of desktop programs.

You might group all messaging apps or Google apps together, for instance. To celebrate work-life balance and detach from work at home or on the weekends, create separate locations for work and personal life.

Multiple Services & Accounts

Easily move between numerous web services and accounts at once with just a click or by using keyboard shortcuts.

Tidy Collections

Access all of your emails, texts, and files in organized, clutter-free settings.
